Progression 0/0

A1 Questions A1

Q1/40. She _____ a teacher.
  • A) am
  • B) is
  • C) are
  • D) be
💡 She → is.
Q2/40. I _____ from Abidjan.
  • A) is
  • B) am
  • C) are
  • D) was
💡 I → am.
Q3/40. What is _____ name?
  • A) you
  • B) your
  • C) yours
  • D) you're
💡 Possessif → your.
Q4/40. There _____ two books on the table.
  • A) is
  • B) are
  • C) has
  • D) have
💡 Pluriel → are.
Q5/40. He _____ coffee every morning.
  • A) drink
  • B) drinks
  • C) drinking
  • D) is drink
💡 He → drinks.
Q6/40. _____ you like football?
  • A) Do
  • B) Does
  • C) Are
  • D) Is
💡 You → Do you...?
Q7/40. We live _____ Cocody.
  • A) at
  • B) in
  • C) on
  • D) to
💡 Ville/quartier → in.
Q8/40. This is _____ orange.
  • A) a
  • B) an
  • C) the
  • D) --
💡 Voyelle → an.

A2 Questions A2

Q9/40. She _____ TV when I called.
  • A) watched
  • B) was watching
  • C) watches
  • D) is watching
💡 Action en cours au passé → was watching.
Q10/40. He is _____ than his brother.
  • A) more tall
  • B) taller
  • C) tallest
  • D) most tall
💡 Comparatif → taller.
Q11/40. They _____ to the cinema last night.
  • A) go
  • B) went
  • C) gone
  • D) going
💡 Last night → went.
Q12/40. You _____ smoke in the hospital.
  • A) don't have to
  • B) mustn't
  • C) shouldn't to
  • D) can
💡 Interdiction → mustn't.
Q13/40. I'm going _____ buy a new phone.
  • A) for
  • B) to
  • C) --
  • D) at
💡 Going to + infinitif.
Q14/40. How _____ water do you drink per day?
  • A) many
  • B) much
  • C) lot
  • D) few
💡 Indénombrable → much.
Q15/40. She has _____ been to London.
  • A) ever
  • B) never
  • C) already
  • D) yet
💡 Absence d'expérience → never.
Q16/40. The restaurant is _____ the bank and the pharmacy.
  • A) among
  • B) between
  • C) next
  • D) behind
💡 Entre deux → between.

B1 Questions B1

Q17/40. I've lived here _____ 2018.
  • A) for
  • B) since
  • C) during
  • D) from
💡 Point précis → since.
Q18/40. The report _____ by the manager yesterday.
  • A) wrote
  • B) was written
  • C) was wrote
  • D) has written
💡 Passif passé → was written.
Q19/40. If it rains, I _____ at home.
  • A) stay
  • B) will stay
  • C) would stay
  • D) stayed
💡 Type 1 → will stay.
Q20/40. She asked me _____ I wanted to join.
  • A) that
  • B) if
  • C) what
  • D) do
💡 Discours indirect → if.
Q21/40. I'm not used to _____ early.
  • A) wake
  • B) waking
  • C) woke
  • D) to wake
💡 Used to + -ing.
Q22/40. The woman _____ car was stolen called the police.
  • A) who
  • B) whose
  • C) which
  • D) that
💡 Possession → whose.
Q23/40. You'd better _____ a jacket.
  • A) to take
  • B) take
  • C) taking
  • D) took
💡 Had better + base verbale.
Q24/40. By the time we arrived, the film _____.
  • A) already started
  • B) had already started
  • C) has started
  • D) was starting
💡 Past perfect → had already started.

B2 Questions B2

Q25/40. If I _____ you, I would accept the offer.
  • A) am
  • B) were
  • C) would be
  • D) had been
💡 Type 2 → were.
Q26/40. Not only _____ the project on time, but she exceeded expectations.
  • A) she completed
  • B) did she complete
  • C) she did complete
  • D) has she completed
💡 Inversion après Not only.
Q27/40. He denied _____ the confidential documents.
  • A) to leak
  • B) having leaked
  • C) leak
  • D) to have leaked
💡 Deny + gérondif parfait.
Q28/40. The deadline needs to be _____ to next Friday.
  • A) put off
  • B) pushed back
  • C) taken away
  • D) called off
💡 Repousser → pushed back.
Q29/40. _____ the weather had been terrible, the event was a success.
  • A) Despite
  • B) Although
  • C) However
  • D) In spite
💡 Although + clause.
Q30/40. She _____ the report if she had had more time.
  • A) will finish
  • B) would have finished
  • C) would finish
  • D) had finished
💡 Type 3 → would have finished.
Q31/40. The manager insisted that every employee _____ the training.
  • A) attends
  • B) attend
  • C) attended
  • D) would attend
💡 Subjonctif → attend.
Q32/40. I wish I _____ harder at university.
  • A) studied
  • B) had studied
  • C) would study
  • D) have studied
💡 Regret passé → had studied.

C1 Questions C1

Q33/40. Rarely _____ such a compelling argument.
  • A) I have heard
  • B) have I heard
  • C) I heard
  • D) did I hear
💡 Inversion avec adverbe négatif.
Q34/40. The proposal, _____ merits are undeniable, was rejected.
  • A) which
  • B) whose
  • C) that
  • D) of which
💡 Possession → whose.
Q35/40. Had the government acted sooner, the crisis _____.
  • A) would be
  • B) could have been averted
  • C) had been
  • D) will have been
💡 Type 3 inversé.
Q36/40. The findings are _____ with previous research.
  • A) coherent to
  • B) consistent with
  • C) relevant for
  • D) compliant to
💡 Consistent with = conforme à.
Q37/40. It is imperative that the board _____ a decision.
  • A) makes
  • B) make
  • C) will make
  • D) made
💡 Subjonctif après imperative.
Q38/40. The CEO, _____ to have been aware, resigned.
  • A) who alleged
  • B) alleged
  • C) alleging
  • D) was alleged
💡 Participe passé = alleged.
Q39/40. _____ notwithstanding, the project proceeded.
  • A) The objections
  • B) Despite objections
  • C) Although objections
  • D) However objections
💡 Noun + notwithstanding.
Q40/40. So thorough was the investigation that no stone _____.
  • A) left unturned
  • B) was left unturned
  • C) had left
  • D) has been unturned
💡 Idiome + inversion.
